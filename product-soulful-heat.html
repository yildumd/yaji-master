Soulful Heat

Balanced, Everyday Yaji

4.5 (128 reviews)

A perfectly balanced blend with medium heat and rich flavor. The citrus note from dried lemon zest makes it uniquely versatile for everyday meals.

Ingredients

  • Dried chilli pepper
  • Roasted groundnut (kuli kuli powder)

  • Ginger powder
  • Lemon zest (dried)

Why This Works

Balanced heat (not aggressive) that enhances without overwhelming. The citrus note makes it unique among yaji blends. Very versatile for meat, fish, chicken, and even fries. Perfect for households and first-time buyers exploring authentic Nigerian flavor.
