Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver outside Abuja?

Yes! We deliver nationwide in Nigeria via courier services. International shipping to diaspora customers is also available.

How long does delivery take?

Abuja: Same-day for orders before 3PM. Other cities: 2-4 business days via courier.

Can I pay on delivery?

Yes, cash on delivery is available for Abuja orders. Other cities require prepayment via bank transfer.
